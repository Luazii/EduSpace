# 🌌 LearniVerse Operations Guide
**Version 1.0 • Hybrid Educational Ecosystem**

Welcome to the official operations manual for LearniVerse. This guide details the platform's workflows from the perspective of every institutional member, ensuring a seamless transition to our premium hybrid school model.

---

## 🧑‍🎓 1. The Student Perspective
*Objective: Mastering the curriculum and attending virtual campus.*

### 🚀 Application & Enrollment
1.  **Admission Application**: Access the landing page and submit your formal application. (Parents usually handle this for younger students).
2.  **Course Dashboard**: Once approved by an Admin, your `/dashboard` will populate with your enrolled courses.
3.  **Qualification Track**: View your assigned Qualifications (e.g., NSC) and see which subjects are required for completion.

### 📚 Learning & Assessments
1.  **Virtual Classroom**: 
    *   Navigate to a **Course Detail** page.
    *   Look for the **"Live Now"** indicator in the header. Click **Join Live Now** to enter the virtual lecture (Zoom/Meet/Jitsi).
    *   View the **Live Classes** tab for upcoming weekly schedules.
2.  **Resources**: Download lecture notes, PDFs, and videos from the **Resources** tab.
3.  **Assignments**: Submit your work through the **Assignments** panel. Track your status from "Pending" to "Graded".
4.  **Quizzes**: Take interactive, timed quizzes. Results are computed instantly upon submission.

---

## 👨‍👩‍👧 2. The Parent Perspective
*Objective: Performance oversight and institutional advocacy.*

### 🔍 Academic Oversight
1.  **Parent Dashboard**: View all students linked to your account at `/parent/dashboard`.
2.  **Achievement Reports**: Click on a student to see their **Performance Report**.
    *   Review **Final Marks** and weighted averages.
    *   Read **Teacher Observations** for each subject.
3.  **Parental Commentary**: Use the **"Leave Comment"** feature on any final mark record to acknowledge results or provide feedback to the school.

### 💬 Institutional Communication
1.  **Engagement & Meetings**: View invitations for Parent-Teacher conferences. 
    *   Action: Click **Confirm Attendance** to formally RSVP to a meeting.
2.  **School Notices**: Read high-priority announcements from the administration (e.g., term dates, emergencies) in your sidebar.
3.  **Financials**: Receive notifications for enrollment fees and make secure payments via the Paystack integration.

---

## 🎓 3. The Teacher Perspective
*Objective: Educational delivery and academic auditing.*

### 👨‍🏫 Classroom Management
1.  **Course Authoring**: 
    *   Upload learning materials to the **Resources** tab.
    *   Create **Assignments** and **Quizzes** with specific deadlines and max marks.
2.  **Live Delivery**: 
    *   Use the **Live Sessions** panel to schedule upcoming online lectures.
    *   Start classes by providing the Meeting URL to students.

### 📝 Grading & Feedback
1.  **Gradebook Integration**: Use the **Teacher Dashboard** to see which students have pending submissions.
2.  **Mark Capture**: Grade assignments and provide qualitative feedback.
3.  **Finalization**: Once a term ends, enter **Final Marks** and summaries. These are instantly pushed to the Parent Hub for review.

---

## 🛡️ 4. The Administrator Perspective
*Objective: Institutional logistics and financial oversight.*

### 👥 Member Management
1.  **User Registry**: Manage all accounts at `/admin/users`. 
    *   Assign roles: **Student, Teacher, Parent, Admin**.
2.  **Parent-Student Linking**: Use the **Parent Links** tool to formally associate guardians with students. *This is a security prerequisite for report access.*

### 📈 Institutional Logistics
1.  **Curriculum Control**: Configure **Faculties, Qualifications, and Courses**. Define which subjects belong to which certificate.
2.  **Communication Hub**: 
    *   **Broadcast Notices**: Send role-specific announcements (e.g., "Teachers Only" or "All Parents").
    *   **Meeting Scheduler**: Coordinate formal engagements between staff and families.
3.  **Financial Audit**: Monitor enrollment revenue and verify transaction statuses through the **Paystack Audit Log**.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Support & Access
*   **Authentication**: All users must sign in via **Clerk** (Email/Social).
*   **Design System**: The platform utilizes the **AntiGravity** high-contrast UI for maximum readability and a premium "Virtual Campus" feel.
*   **Real-time Updates**: LearniVerse uses **Convex** for live synchronization. Marks, messages, and live session statuses update instantly without refreshing the page.
